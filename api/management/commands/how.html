How Can We Get in on This?

SPEAK, WRITE, DRAW/LISTEN, READ, SEE

We want people who live, work or play on the island to weigh in on critical questions about race, immigration, gentrification and inclusion. We want to hear your crazy imaginings of how we can make this a more a dynamic inclusive community that keeps it real.

Currently we are accepting submissions via email at GetOutIsland@gmail.com. An upload function will be coming soon. You can also reach out to us via email if you would prefer to set up an interview with a volunteer on our team.

Keep it simple. You can film a dance on your smartphone, send us a poem, painting or essay. Be creative. We will accept all submissions that meet the Ground Rules. All ages are welcome (consent forms must be signed by parents for minors under 18 years of age).

Please Note: WordToRI.com is not affiliated with any formal organization. Viewing NYC produced the Blackwell Island Lighthouse video featured above.
